Creative Direction

Cadbury wanted a sensory visual journey that echoed the indulgence of their brand. Our concept: flood the entire screen with chocolate and milk to build anticipation and unveil the product with cinematic simplicity. Each moment was crafted to delay gratification—aligning with Cadbury’s narrative of savoring sweet moments.

Challenges

We explored multiple passes of fluid simulation to find the perfect balance of weight, viscosity, and motion. The interaction of milk and chocolate had to remain visually readable, satisfying, and brand-accurate even at billboard scale.

Execution Breakdown

• Blender powered the fluid dynamics and rich chocolate textures
• Cinema 4D refined projection path and animation timing
After Effects polished the mood and ensured brand-consistent color grading

•Final delivery within a 4-week turnaround

Frequently asked questions

Clients Ask Us Most >>

What makes a billboard "anamorphic"?

It’s a 3D illusion technique using forced perspective. We align the camera angle and viewer’s line of sight to create the illusion of objects popping out of the screen.

Can I see a preview before full production?

Yes, every project starts with a 3D animatic so you see motion, framing, and concept early on.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on length and complexity, but typically ranges from $8k to $25k per billboard-ready creative.

Do you support international billboard formats?

Absolutely. We’ve delivered for screens in UAE, UK, US, and Asia, adapting to every pixel grid, resolution, and screen curve.
