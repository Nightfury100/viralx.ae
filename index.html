Frequently asked questions

Clear answers to everything you need to know about 3D billboards, CGI ads, and immersive brand experiences.

How long does a 3D billboard or motion ad take to create?

Typically 2–4 weeks, depending on the complexity, animation length, and feedback cycles. For urgent campaigns, we offer accelerated delivery options.

Do I need to provide the concept or storyboard?

No our team handles everything end-to-end, including concept development, scripting, storyboarding, 3D modeling, animation, and delivery. You just bring the brand goals.

What do you need from us to get started?

A quick call to understand your campaign objective, logo brand assets, any product references, and your target location or platform (LED screen, social media, etc.). We’ll take it from there.

Can you adapt the creative for different screen sizes or platforms?

Yes. We design with flexibility and export presets, so your project can scale to fit curved LED screens, vertical reels, DOOH, or in-store displays—without needing a full rework.

Can you work with our media agency or outdoor vendor?

Absolutely. We collaborate smoothly with media buyers, OOH vendors, or city permit teams to ensure the output meets all technical specs and timeline requirements.

Do you handle installation or on-site calibration?

Yes, when needed. For projection mapping or custom LED rigs, we support tech checks, site surveys, and animation calibration especially for curved or cornered screens.

How much does a typical 3D billboard campaign cost?

Pricing starts around $6,000–10,000 USD for a single location-ready asset, but varies by scope (duration, complexity, formats). We offer fixed project rates with transparent breakdowns.

Can I reuse the visuals for social media or other ads?

Yes, and we recommend it. Many clients repurpose the 3D content into reels, ad banners, display loops, and paid campaign assets to stretch ROI across channels.

Is this the same as AI or template animation?

No our work is fully custom CGI & motion design, built by human artists + technical directors. No auto-generated templates. No generic assets.

How do I get started?

Click “Schedule Concept Call” or “Claim Reel Draft” on any of our service pages, and our creative team will get back to you within 24 hours.
